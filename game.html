Game Mode

In game mode, you have to guess the city name of the map displayed.

To submit your answer, select your guess from the drop down menu of city names. (Note: you will only have ONE guess, the first city you click in the drop down menu will be submitted as your answer

Amateur mode: The complete map will be displayed excluding any text (E.g. street names)

Intermediate mode: A partially complete map will be displayed excluding any text and streets

Professional mode: Only the transit system and land features of the city will be displayed
